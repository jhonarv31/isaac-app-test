About

Instructional Solution and Assistance for Computers was designed to give assistance for people who are having problems in troubleshooting a computer. It can only run on Android phones having KitKat, Lollipop, and Marshmallow as its mobile operating system.

On one hand, the application can be accessed offline. On the other hand, if the application would updated, considering all information present in the application, it requires Internet connection. The topics to be included will be based from the different Computer Hardware Servicing NC II modules of Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) which are Maintaining Computer Systems, and Networks Diagnosing, and Troubleshooting Computer Systems, Installing of Computer Systems, and Networks, and Computer Troubleshooting, and Networks.

However, it cannot give solution to software problems, and at the same time, if ever the given solution did not work, the user must consult an expert or computer repair technician.
